Health Tips

1. Balanced Diet: Ensure that your child maintains a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. A well-rounded diet provides essential nutrients for growth and development.

2. Regular Exercise: Encourage your child to engage in regular physical activity. Playtime, sports, and outdoor activities not only keep them active but also support their overall health.

3. Vaccination Schedule: Stay updated on your child's vaccination schedule. Vaccinations are crucial in preventing various diseases and ensuring your child's immune system is strong.

4. Hydration: Keep your child hydrated by ensuring they drink an adequate amount of water throughout the day. Proper hydration is essential for bodily functions and overall well-being.
